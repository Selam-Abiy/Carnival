Operation hotel layout

  1. 1. INTRODUCTION • Spatial location • Comparison with other industries • Appropriate location is paramount • Influences a tourist’s hotel selection decision.
  2. 2. FACTORS OF PLANT LOCATION PRIMARY CHEAPER MATERIALS CHEAPER LABOUR LESS POWER CHEAP TRANSPORTATION PROXIMITY SECONDARY SUITABLE CLIMATIC CONDITIONS INDUSTRIAL PEACE INFRASTRUCTURE FACILITES GOVERNMENT SUPPORT COST OF LAND
  3. 3. POTENTIAL DETERMINANTS  LOCATION ATTRIBUTES  INDIVIDUAL CHARACTERISTICS
  4. 4. LOCATION ATTRIBUTES • ACCESSIBILITY • AGGLOMERATION EFFECT • PUBLIC GOODS AND SERVICES • URBAN DEVELOPMENT
  5. 5. INDIVIDUAL ATTRIBUTES • SCALE • STAR RATING • OWNERSHIP • SERVICE DIVERSIFICATION
  6. 6. OTHER FACTORS • LOBBY • TECHNOLOGY • BEDS AND BEEDINGS • CASE-GOODS • LIGHTING
  7. 7. CONCLUSION  The proposed place for starting a business or a place where the business is situated is termed as location.  Locate a site with minimized cost.  Various benefits with each region. Available government support.  Location near transport links. Advantage over competitors.
  8. 8. PPC CYCLE 1ST PHASE  Preplanning Phase 1. Product Design Development 2. Sales Forecasting & Estimating 3. Factory Layout Design & Preparation 4. Equipment Selection Policy Decision 5. Preplanning of production just prior to large scale production
  9. 9. PPC CYCLE 2ND PHASE o Planning of 4 M’s 1. Method 2. Material 3. Man 4. Machines o Routing o Estimating o Scheduling o Dispatching
  10. 10. PPC CYCLE 3RD PHASE  Follow up or Chasing  Inspection  Evaluation
  11. 11. PPC CYCLE FOR HOTEL  Deciding the type and location of hotel - The first step in starting a hotel is deciding what sort of an enterprise it is going to be. It could be a business hotel or one catering primarily to tourists.  Logistics of the Hotel - It is important that the hotel site is near important transit points such as railway stations and airports
  12. 12.  Planning the hotel layout - need to get professional assistance for planning the hotel’s layout  Finances of the Hotel - Before starting the hotel, the owners should make sure that the financial requirements are well satisfied  Market surveys for the Hotel - The owners should hire professional agencies to do a survey for finding out the business potential in a particular area. Ideally the whole exercise should be done in a radius of 2 to 10 kms
  13. 13.  Getting licenses for the Hotel - Majority of these have to be renewed on a yearly basis and the most important among these are applicable for lodging, liquor, water and sewage, and food  Machine – For a hotel machine is a hotel itself normally a facility with 70-120 rooms needs an approximate area ranging from 10 thousand to 60 thousand square feet. The area needed is also determined by the quality of the hotel.
  14. 14.  Recruitment for the Hotel (Man) While starting the hotel, the owners should also look to recruit with an emphasis on the following positions:  Customer care executives  Sweepers  Managers  Cleaners  Housekeepers  Washers  Waiters
  15. 15.  Controlling – On an experimental bases for first 1 month some discount will be offered and study the customer requirement and liking and dis-liking  Evaluating – at evaluating stage we find out whether the hotel is functioning as per the planning and if it is found that it is working as per our expectation then it will be continued for long time.
